Our work on "Software Engineering Educational Experience in Building an Intelligent Tutoring System" has been accepted for [SEET@ICSE 2024](https://conf.researchr.org/track/icse-2024/icse-2024-software-engineering-education-and-training-track)!
We present a teaching concept that involves the incremental development of an Intelligent Tutoring System (ITS) spanning multiple semesters and share our experience with the student engagements. You can find the pre-print on [Arxiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2310.05472.pdf).
