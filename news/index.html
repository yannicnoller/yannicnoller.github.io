Dec 15, 2023 Our work on “Software Engineering Educational Experience in Building an Intelligent Tutoring System” has been accepted for SEET@ICSE 2024! We present a teaching concept that involves the incremental development of an Intelligent Tutoring System (ITS) spanning multiple semesters and share our experience with the student engagements. You can find the pre-print on Arxiv.
